I am an assistant professor at the School of Cyber Science and Technology,the Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing at the Beihang University.

My research areas are mainly big data analysis and artificial intelligence, social data analysis and mining, spatio-temporal data analysis and mining. My research often uses deep learning, data mining, text mining, and spatiotemporal prediction as design methods. My work draws from,combines and contributes to the areas of social network mining, social text mining, and anomaly detection.This is a system developed by my PhD: Big Data Event Detection and Analysis Computing Platform

I am a recipient of the grand class of Technological Invention Award of Chinese Institute of Electronics, 2018; the first prize of excellent scientific research of National Computer Network Emergency Technology Processing and Coordination Center,2017;National scholarship for doctoral students,2017
