Hao Peng

彭浩

I am an assistant professor at the School of Cyber Science and Technology, and also a researcher at the Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing at the Beihang University.

I aim to make artificial intelligence technology easier and more accessible in industry. My broad research interests include big data analysis and artificial intelligence, social data representation, spatio-temporal data prediction, and industrial data mining and system. My work often uses machine learning as a design methodology.
