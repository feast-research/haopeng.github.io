Hao Peng

彭浩

I am an assistant professor at the School of Cyber Science and Technology, and also a researcher at the Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing at the Beihang University.

I aim to make artificial intelligence technology easier and more accessible in industry. My broad research interests include big data analysis and artificial intelligence, social data representation, spatio-temporal data prediction, and industrial data mining. My work often uses machine learning as a design methodology.

I am a recipient of the excellent doctor degree dissertation of Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence,2020;the outstanding graduates of Beihang University,2019;the grand class of Technological Invention Award of Chinese Institute of Electronics, 2018; the first prize of excellent scientific research of National Computer Network Emergency Technology Processing and Coordination Center,2017;National scholarship for doctoral students,2017
